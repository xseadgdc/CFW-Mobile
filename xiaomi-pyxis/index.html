Xiaomi Mi CC 9 / Mi 9 Lite (pyxis)

Xiaomi Mi CC 9 / Mi 9 Lite is powered by the Qualcomm SDM710 Snapdragon 710 CPU and equipped with the Qualcomm Adreno 616 GPU. It has 6 GB of RAM and 64/128 GB of internal storage. Its Li-Po battery is non-removable and has a capacity of 4030 mAh. It was released in September 2019.
