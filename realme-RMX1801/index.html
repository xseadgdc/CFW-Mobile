Realme 2 Pro (RMX1801)

Realme 2 Pro is powered by the Qualcomm SDM660 Snapdragon 660 CPU and equipped with the Qualcomm Adreno 512 GPU. It has 4/6/8 GB LPDDR4X of RAM and 64/128 GB eMMC5.1 of internal storage. Its Li-Ion battery is non-removable and has a capacity of 3500 mAh. It was released on October 11, 2018.
